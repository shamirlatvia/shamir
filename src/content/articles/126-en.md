The concert “Mir singen Yiddish” took place on September 18 at 18:00 in the White Hall of Riga Latvian Society house (Merķeļa Street 13) and on September 20 at 18:00 in Daugavpils music college (Kandavas Street 2a). Followers of Nechama Lifshitsayte, famous singer and fighter for Jews rights in Soviet Union, from Israel performed folk and author songs in Yiddish, as well as Ghetto and Jewish Resistance songs. Participating Gali Davidov, Rose Klein, Irina Kopitova, Regina Dricker, piano, and Martha Smith (Norway). Traditionally Riga concert is only for women.
