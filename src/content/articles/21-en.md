Concert of cantors’ and Jewish folk music on September 06, 2012 in Splendid palace (Elizabetes str. 61). Tickets – www.bilesuserviss.lv. Jewish prayers and folk songs, traditional melodies and exciting niguns. Young cantors from the Tel Aviv&nbsp;Cantorial&nbsp;Institute gave a wonderful concert in&nbsp;Riga&nbsp;two years ago, and now they will bring new program here. Performing Raymond Goldstein (Piano), Aharon Gerlitz, Avraham Bernholtz, Daniel Colthof (Deputy Chief Cantor Of Z.H.L. – Army of&nbsp;Israel).
