The Riga Ghetto and Latvian Holocaust Museum has officially joined ICOM Latvia — the national committee of the International Council of Museums. ICOM is the world’s leading organization for museum professionals, with over 50,000 members across 138 countries. It sets global standards for ethics, practice, and collaboration in the museum field.

Becoming a member of ICOM connects our museum with an international network of professionals, educational resources, and collaboration opportunities. It is a significant recognition of the museum’s professional work and an important step toward deeper international cooperation and developmen.
