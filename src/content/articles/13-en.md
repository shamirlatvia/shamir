Association “Shamir” and Riga Ghetto and Latvian Holocaust museum kindly invites you to take part in the ceremony of the official opening of the exhibition “Jews in North Africa during World War II”. The event will be held at **Riga Ghetto and Latvian Holocaust Museum:** **Turgeneva street 2, hall 9** (entrance from Krasta street) on **June 27, 2019** at **15:00**

This topic will be represented for the first time in Europe. The exhibition was prepared by the Israeli Ben-Zvi Institute. The exhibition is available in French, English, Latvian, Russian and Hebrew. The exhibition tells about the unknown historical aspects of the Holocaust in the Maghreb countries: Tunisia, Morocco, Libya, Algeria.
