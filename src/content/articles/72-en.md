Exhibition of three Hamburg and Moscow artists Roman Feierstein, Lubov Simonenko and Anna Simonenko is to be opened on September 12, 2011 at 16:00 in Latvian State History archive. The exhibition is historical as well as artistic one. That is exhibition-dialogue, dialogue of epochs, of cultures and of spaces. Exhibition lasts till October 28, 2011.
