Final&nbsp; accord&nbsp; of&nbsp; the&nbsp; Jewish culture festival 5771 – since September 27th&nbsp;&nbsp; unique&nbsp; exhibition,&nbsp; „Flying&nbsp; Letters”&nbsp; at&nbsp; the&nbsp; House&nbsp; of&nbsp; the Blackheads will take place. Well-known Israeli sculptress Aviva Shemer [www.avivart.com](http://www.avivart.com/),&nbsp;&nbsp; Latvian&nbsp; artist&nbsp; Jekaterina&nbsp; Beļajeva&nbsp; and&nbsp; Israeli photographer&nbsp; Dani&nbsp; Dolev&nbsp; will participate. Each letter of the Hebrew alphabet&nbsp; has&nbsp; its&nbsp; kabbalistic&nbsp; meaning.

Learn&nbsp; about this and other interesting&nbsp; facts&nbsp; when&nbsp; you visit the House of the Blackheads. Aviva Shemer’s&nbsp; paintings&nbsp; will be exhibited through October 4th after which it&nbsp; will&nbsp; travel&nbsp; to&nbsp; South&nbsp; Korea.&nbsp; All&nbsp; other exhibits will be shown through October 24th.
