„Cabaret a la Yiddish” – a unique concert for the Jewish community! The show’s visionary and lead soloist is the internationally famous Aelita (from the USA). Performing with her Riga’s rising young star Diana Judelson and the dance ensemble „Eilat”. Only women performers and audience members.

At concert “60 minutes around the world” famous Aelita from USA invited her guests to participate in the round-the-world trip, singing her songs in 16 languages including Yiddish. Actors of the theater “Obseravtory”&nbsp;were&nbsp;also&nbsp;performing.
