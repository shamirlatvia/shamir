We are happy to present you a catalogue of Shamir Society Publishing house. The catalogue, published by the Association “Shamir” over the last 15 years, contains books, maps, and albums about the history of Jews in Latvia and Jewish culture and religion. All these books are available in the information center of the Riga Ghetto and Latvian Holocaust Museum. They can also be purchased online. If you wish to purchase the book, please contact us at project@shamir.lv The catalogue is available at the following link: [A catalogue of the “Šamir” publishing house](https://clck.ru/HAfak)
