Concert of liturgical music, dedicated to New Jewish year took place on September 14, 2011 at 19:00 in the Great Hall of Riga Latvian Society house (Merķeļa Str. 13). Participating cantor Baruch Finkelstein and the male group of choir “Balsis”, conductor Ints Teterovskis, accompanied by Jevgeny Hazdan, piano. Jewish liturgical and folk music was performed.
