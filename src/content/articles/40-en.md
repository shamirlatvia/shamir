September 4-6 in Riga happened Riga Forum – Holocaust and Modern Radicalism. More than 100 participants from 17 countries participated in Forum. It was discovered topics about questions of history and modern issues. Participants shared their experience and presented current projects. Forum include 2 events – 5th International Conference “Holocaust Museums and memorial places in postcommunist countries” and 2nd Round table about modern issues of Tolerance. Participants of forum are scientists, teachers, politics, and journalists. As always, the Forum became a place for new contacts and new cooperation.
