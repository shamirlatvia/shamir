Performance for children “Gefilte Lid” took place on September 13, 2009 in &nbsp;Riga Jewish Community House

Duo «Oyfn Veg» (Moscow): Jevgenija Slavina and Igor Beliy. Interactive performance for children and adults – plays, songs, mysteries and folk tales. Old tailor and his donkey are going to the fair to buy a fish for the Saturday meals.
