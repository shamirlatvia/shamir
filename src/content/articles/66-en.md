By the initiative of Jewish Association “Shamir” and Riga Ghetto and Holocaust museum special limited edition of the postmark in memory of the Holocaust in Latvia was issued. Drawing by Joseph Kuzkovsky. The postmark is available at Association “Shamir”, Riga, Stabu 63, ph. +371-67270827. Income will be directed to the development of Riga Ghetto and Latvian Holocaust museum.
