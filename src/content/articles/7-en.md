On the 1st February 2010 the first group from Latvia, consisted of 25 history teachers, arrived at the memorial Yad Vashem in Jerusalem for ten days long seminar about the Holocaust. The project was realized in cooperation with the Ministry of Education and Science of Latvia and with the support of the company airBaltic. The program of seminar consisted of lectures on the history of the Jews and the Holocaust from the best Israel researchers, as well as excursions around the country and Jerusalem.
