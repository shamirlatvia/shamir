Within the framework of the project of the multimedia exhibition “Marc Chagall’s color music”, **[a virtual tour of the exhibition](http://rgmvirtualtours.lv/)** was created.

The creation of the virtual tour was supported by the Education, Culture, and Sports Department of Riga City Council (RD IKSD), as well as LNK Charity Fund, LG Latvia. Materials for the exhibition were provided by the Latvian National Museum of Art, Boris Lurie Art Foundation, and private collectors.
