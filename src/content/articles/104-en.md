Mark Marjanovski memorial concert took place in Riga on May 10, 2012. Mark Marjanovski’s tangos are forgotten and he himself perished in Buchenwald in 1944. This concert is an another attempt to save Latvian Jewish heritage. Performing Latvian singer and actor Richard Leper and Nikolay Bochkun, piano – Tom Juhnevich and Jury Kasper, idea and supervision – Marina Mihailets.
